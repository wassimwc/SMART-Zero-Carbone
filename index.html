Smart Monitoring for a Sustainable Future

SMART Zero Carbone is an IoT-based system that improves workplace conditions and energy efficiency. It monitors humidity, temperature, air quality, energy consumption, and renewable energy production, adjusting automatically through VHAC. Aligned with global sustainability goals like COP27 and COP28, it helps businesses reduce their carbon footprint and operate more efficiently.
